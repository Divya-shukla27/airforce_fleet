Welcome to the Indian Air Force Fleet

The Indian Air Force (IAF) is one of the world's most potent air forces, entrusted with safeguarding India's skies and maintaining air superiority. Its motto, "Nabha Sparsham Deeptham," translates to "Touch the Sky with Glory." The IAF is dedicated to defending the nation with pride and ensuring national security through cutting-edge air technologies and precision in operations.

Operations

Operation Vijay

Operation Vijay was an aerial strike operation undertaken during the Kargil War to counter enemy intrusions and ensure the safety of Indian borders.

Operation Meghdoot

Operation Meghdoot was launched for the deployment of troops and supplies to high-altitude posts along the Siachen Glacier, making it one of the most successful military operations in extreme conditions.

Operation Cactus

Operation Cactus was a military intervention in the Maldives to restore the country's government during a coup attempt, demonstrating the Indian Air Force's swift response to regional security concerns.

Operation Poomalai

Operation Poomalai was a humanitarian mission by the Indian Air Force to drop supplies to the Tamil people in Sri Lanka during the civil war, aiding the Sri Lankan Tamil refugees.

Operation Rahat

During the flood relief operations in Uttarakhand IAF responded to this enormous challenge with characteristic speed, resolve and fortitude. On 17 Jun 13, the IAF commenced operations and as reports of the scale of disaster started pouring in; IAF within 48 hours, positioned 45 heptrs of eight different types, operating from eight different locations. Two forward focal points were set up at Dharasu and Gauchar.

Operation Shakti

Operation Shakti was the codename for India's 1998 nuclear tests in Pokhran, which were supported by the Indian Air Force to ensure the security of the test site and airspace.

Operation Trident

Operation Trident was a naval and air operation during the 1971 Indo-Pakistani War that involved the Indian Navy and the IAF conducting airstrikes on Karachi's port facilities and naval targets.

Operation Black Tornado

Operation Black Tornado was the operation carried out by the Indian Air Force to rescue hostages during the 26/11 Mumbai attacks. The operation involved IAF helicopters for troop deployment and rescue operations.

Fighter Jets

Sukhoi Su-30MKI

A multirole air superiority fighter designed to perform air-to-air and air-to-ground combat. Used extensively in air defense and offensive missions.

Dassault Rafale

A modern, versatile multirole fighter aircraft used for air superiority, ground attacks, and deep strike missions.

MiG-29

Used for air superiority, interdiction, and precision bombing; equipped for both aerial combat and attack missions.

Mirage 2000

A French-origin fighter aircraft known for its agility, used in deep strike operations and precision bombing.

Tejas

An indigenously developed light multirole aircraft designed to be a cost-effective and versatile solution for air defense and strike roles.

MiG-21

An older, but still operational, aircraft primarily used for air defense and intercepting intruders.

Jaguar

A ground attack aircraft used for strike operations, including precision bombing and targeting enemy positions.

Cargo Planes

C-17 Globemaster III

A heavy-lift transport aircraft capable of carrying oversized cargo, used for strategic deployment of heavy equipment and troops.

C-130 Hercules

A versatile tactical transport aircraft used for airlift missions, including rapid deployment of troops, humanitarian aid, and special operations.

Antonov An-32

A medium-lift cargo aircraft used for transporting personnel, supplies, and equipment to remote areas with short runways.

IL-76

A large military transport aircraft used for long-range heavy-lift missions, transporting cargo, troops, and equipment.

Airbus A330 MRTT

Aerial refueling aircraft capable of transferring fuel to other aircraft mid-air and transporting cargo or passengers.

Helicopters

Mi-17 V5

A versatile transport and utility helicopter used for troop transport, disaster relief, and logistical support.

Apache AH-64E

A combat helicopter designed for attack missions, equipped with advanced targeting and weapon systems for ground and air combat.

Chinook CH-47F

A heavy-lift tandem rotor helicopter used for transporting heavy loads, troops, and equipment.

Dhruv

An indigenously developed utility helicopter used for reconnaissance, troop transport, and medical evacuation operations.
